Francesca Dori also called "Chicca" is an Italian interior designer and photographer based in London. She has a degree in Interior Design from the University of Florence and a Master in Interior Design from the University of Westminster. She has worked for several interior design studios in London and Florence, and has collaborated with several architects and interior designers. She has also worked as a freelance photographer for several interior design magazines and companies. She is currently working on her own projects and is looking for new collaborations.
